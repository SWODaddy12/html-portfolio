The Best Movies According to Tom

My top 3 movies of all time.

Braveheart

A great display of what a powerful effect one can have on others when you have a single minded focus on what is right.

We Were Soldiers

An outstanding portrayal of effective leadership, especially when having to work without the tools, resources, and proper preparation to do the job.

The Secret Life of Walter Mitty

A beautiful film about getting out of your own head and enjoying life in the moment.
